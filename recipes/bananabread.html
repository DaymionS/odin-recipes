Joy's Easy Banana Bread

Description

This delicious and easy banana bread recipe comes together quickly

with budget-friendly ingredients you likely already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)
  2. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan
  3. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together in a bowl
  4. Mix flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl
  5. Stir into banana mixture until batter is just mixed
  6. Stir in salt
  7. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of the bread comes out clean, about 1 hour
  9. Enjoy
