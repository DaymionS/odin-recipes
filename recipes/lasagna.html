Easy Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves a lot of time. This dish satisfies the family. Try it.

Making homemade lasagna doesn't have to be tedious. This recipe come together quickly with a short ingredient list.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 32 ounce jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 8 to 10 minutes
  4. Drain and discard grease
  5. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated Parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl
  7. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish
  8. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce
  9. Repeat layers once more
  10. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese
  11. Pour 1/2 cup water along the edges of the dish
  12. Cover tightly with aluminum foil
  13. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes
  14. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes
  15. Let stand 10 minutes before serving
  16. Serve and enjoy
